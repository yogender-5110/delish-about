The Connoisseurs Delishh! specializes in developing frozen appetizers, import and distribution of frozen fruits, vegetable and meat products. Established in 2008, the company is situated in Noida (U.P.), India.

Delishh! takes utmost care in the packaging and storage of its products. Our production unit is equipped with 2 fully operational kitchens, packaging houses, one quick frozen house, an onsite lab, metal detectors and packaging machines.

With a capacity of manufacturing 2500 Kilos of processed food per day, we are still capable of multiplying this at any given short notice due to the availability of a large enough working space and a great team of working staff.

With skilled workers and an experienced management team, Delishh! has forever been able to supply the best quality products at very competitive prices. Our current target markets are India, U.K., Australia and the Middle East.

The Connoisseurs Delishh! is an MFPO, FPO, PFA, ISO 22000 certified company.
